StatusSoap

About Us

StatusSop is a web app dedicated to helping Facebook users tidy up their online presence for job hunting, college applications, family, getting rid of your old embarrassing statuses, or just because...

The Idea

When spring semester senior year came to a close, we saw more and more people changing their names on Facebook to hide from recruiters. We saw friends do massive clean sweeps of their prior posts. And we saw people deleting their Facebooks altogether. This spawned the idea of StatusSoap--a way to assist people in making their social media more suitable for a professional environment.

How it Started

It began as a project for a software engineering design class at the University of Texas at Austin. Although the final prototype in the class was fairly basic and crude, StatusSoap generated a lot of interest from classmates and our teacher. From there, we decided to continue development after the class was finished.
